# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and respectful environment. All participants are expected to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Showing respect and empathy for others
- Considering differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism and feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants:
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language or actions
- Threats, derogatory comments, or insults
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at oss@hanselwei.dev. Reports will be handled with discretion and respect for all parties involved.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.
